About Us

Welcome to Aswath Naturale, where nature meets wellness.

Our Story

At Aswath Naturale, we are passionate about crafting premium, all-natural hair oils and health care wellness products designed to bring out the best in you.

Our journey began with a simple but profound goal: to harness the power of nature’s finest ingredients to enhance holistic health, vitality, and beauty.

Rooted in the healing wisdom of plants and ancient remedies, as taught by great rishis and healers of our culture, we are committed to providing high-quality, effective products that nourish you from the inside out.

Aswath Naturale was founded out of a deep respect for nature's wisdom by Dr. Krishnakumar R, a visionary with over 38 years of experience in health services, education, training, and counselling. Dr. Krishnakumar R comes from a family of doctors, his father being an MD in alternative medicine and a naturopath who practiced the philosophy that “food is medicine.” He believes our bodies are composed of the five elements (Pancha Boothas), and that substances inconsistent with these elements can cause irreversible damage. This profound understanding of various healing systems has enabled him to develop unique products aligned with the mission: “Nature meets Wellness.”

Driven by a passion for holistic healing, Dr. Krishnakumar focuses on providing natural alternatives to modern healthcare systems, which often carry harmful side effects. At Aswath Naturale, we blend time-honoured herbal traditions with cutting-edge scientific advancements to formulate solutions that meet diverse wellness needs — from enhancing hair health and boosting energy to promoting overall well-being.

Dr. Krishnakumar, CEO is ably supported by Dr. Venkatesan Lakshminarasimhan, our Chief Operating Officer and Mentor, whose illustrious career spans over 40 years in education and the corporate sector. Dr Venkatesan is the Honorary Chairman of Computer society of India Coimbatore. He is a philanthropist who is passionate in serving humanity and is the Trustee of Lakshmi Venkatesan Charitable Trust an NGO to serve the community in education and health care. He is also an enthusiast in Green Revolution.

We are ably guided and directed by various industry experts, health care and other professionals from different fields.
